Nastaran: Prepare the Meat: In a large pot, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the onions and saut√© until golden. Add the meat and turmeric, browning the meat on all sides.
Add Herbs and Spices: Add the parsley, coriander, chives, and dried fenugreek to the pot. Stir well until the herbs are fragrant.
Cooking: Pierce the dried limes with a fork and add them to the pot (or add lime juice). Pour in the water, bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Season with salt and pepper.
Simmer: Cover and simmer for about 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the meat is tender and the stew has thickened.
